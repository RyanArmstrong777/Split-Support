Privacy Policy for Split
Effective Date: 13/08/2025
Developer: Ryan Armstrong
Overview
Split is a mobile app developed by Ryan Armstrong. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected when you use the app and how it is handled. We do not collect personal information directly. However, we use third-party services that may collect data as part of their functionality.
Data Collection
Split does not require you to create an account or provide personal information. However, the app uses the following third-party services which may collect data:
1. Google AdMob
Used to serve ads in the app. Google may collect:
- Device information (e.g., advertising ID, device model)
- Usage data
- Approximate location (inferred from IP)
- Ad interaction data
- Crash and performance data
For more details, please refer to:
Google's privacy policy
2. Revenue cat
Used to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect:
- Purchase history
- App user ID (anonymous)
- Device data (e.g., platform, app version)
For more details, visit:
Revenue cat's privacy policy
Data usage
All data collected by third-party services is used to:
- Provide and optimize app functionality (e.g., IAP, subscriptions)
- Serve and improve advertising
- Monitor app performance and crashes
We do not sell or share your personal data.
Tracking and Advertising
If personalized ads are enabled, Google AdMob may use device identifiers and other data to show relevant ads. You can opt out of ad personalization via your device settings:
On iOS: Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising
Children's Privacy
Split is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us.
Changes to This Policy
This policy may be updated occasionally. Changes will be reflected on this page, and the effective date will be updated.
